### Operating Principle: Pain first. Edge second.

**Core messaging hierarchy:** Problem → Cost → Power → Promise.
- **Problem:** The bettor is betting blind / advertised / emotionally / with one bookmaker only.
- **Cost:** Worse prices, lower value, less context, lost Edge before kickoff.
- **Power:** MzansiEdge reads the full picture: bookmakers, pricing, market signals, form, injuries, weather, venue, and more.
- **Promise:** Bet smarter. Read the market better. Find your Edge.

**Selling philosophy: Edges as Treasure.** Edges are not data points — they are shining moments of opportunity most bettors walk past. Our algorithm is the miner. We unearth them and hand them to you. Show the prize (R645 from R300), not the process (+5.7%). Tiers are rarity levels (Diamond shimmers, Bronze is muted). Edges expire at kickoff — scarcity is real, not manufactured. The subscription unlocks the mine. A single Diamond Edge could triple your money — and without us, you'd never have known it existed. Losses don't break the spell — they build trust. Apply to: hero card, pricing, Telegram, ads, social, every conversion moment.

**Tone:** Sharp, premium, informed, punchy, local, opinionated, market-literate, emotionally alive, educational, disciplined. **Never:** generic SaaS, gambling spam, robotic, flat, overhyped, bro-gambler-ish.

**Signature patterns:** Contrast structures (Stop X. Start Y.), wrong diagnosis → real diagnosis, market-reading language (read the market, play the market, priced in, quiet value).

**SA flavour:** Approved (vibes, gees, hectic, proper). Conditional (lekker). Avoid in core copy (bru, china, overdone slang). One SA marker max per post.

**Channel tone ladder:** Website = most premium → X = most provocative → Instagram = authority + save/share → Facebook = fuller educational → Ads = pain → cost → Edge → Telegram = analysis + education + transparency.

**QA before publishing:** (1) Real pain/tension? (2) Distinctly MzansiEdge? (3) Sharp and premium? (4) Edge capitalised? (5) No generic AI/SaaS/spam? (6) Educates/reframes/provokes? (7) Could belong to any betting brand? If yes, rewrite.

### Hero Design Principles (LOCKED — 18 March 2026)

The homepage hero is the gold standard. All sections must aspire to its level. Why it works:

1. **Dual-column tension.** Left = emotional hook (headline, pain, CTAs). Right = product proof (B.R.U., Edge card, results). Left creates desire, right delivers evidence.
2. **B.R.U. is active, not decorative.** He gestures toward the card with a speech bubble. He's the guide delivering treasure. Every B.R.U. placement must be purposeful.
3. **The Edge card is a treasure chest.** R645 is the hero number. Diamond shimmers. Confidence bar gamifies quality. Insight quote explains WHY. It shows what you would have missed.
4. **Recent Edges = proof.** Wins AND losses in Rands. One loss builds more trust than a perfect record.
5. **Founding Member hook is one tight line.** No blocks, no paragraphs.
6. **Dark-mode energy.** Carbon Black bg, stadium texture, orange gradient accents on high-value elements only. 60/30/10 colour rule.
7. **Copy follows the DNA.** Pain first, Edge second. Rhythm. Punch. No feature sludge.
