edge_picks_index_v1.html ✅ Canonical Approved 2026-04-25 · 5 iterations · INV-HOT-TIPS-A2-IMPL-DESIGN-01

Edge Picks tier index card. Image-first surface sent on the hot:go callback. Shows all four tiers (Diamond → Gold → Silver → Bronze) with live counts. Locked tiers show 🔒 + "Upgrade to unlock"; unlocked tiers show a tier-coloured tick to the right of the count. The user's highest-unlocked tier is highlighted as "★ Your default". Five rendered states below — one PNG per (user_tier × locks) combination.

Design notes (locked constraints)

  • Header matches canonical edge_picks.html header: mzansiedge-wordmark-dark-transparent.png at height 32px, orange brand bar (3px gradient #FF6B00→#F7931A→#FFBE45), "EDGE PICKS" title (white, letter-spacing 3px), per-tier count strip 💎N · 🥇N · 🥈N · 🥉N — N edges today with each tier emoji+count colored by canonical tier_color.
  • Tier rows in rank order: Diamond → Gold → Silver → Bronze. Border-left 3px in tier_color for unlocked, neutral grey #2A2A2A for locked.
  • Default highlight on user's highest unlocked tier: gradient bg linear-gradient(90deg, {tier_color}15 0%, #1A1A1A 60%) + orange ★ Your default badge (#F7931A).
  • Locked rows: medal emoji desaturated to filter: grayscale(0.6); opacity: 0.55, background #131313, secondary text colour #B3B3B3, lock icon 🔒 on the right.
  • Unlocked tick: SVG path M4 12.5 L10 18.5 L20 6 at stroke-width: 3.5, rounded caps + joins, stroke="{tier.color}". 22×22 px box, sits to the right of the count block.
  • Live count always vertically aligned: big number (JetBrains Mono, 22px, 700) over small "LIVE" label (uppercase, 9px, letter-spaced). No emoji prefix on the count.
  • Empty state: header strip shows zeros, body shows centred 🌙 + "No live edges right now / Check back soon · we scan every 15 minutes". Tier rows dimmed to opacity: 0.5; unlocked Bronze keeps its tier-colour tick (access semantics independent of count).
  • Footer: mzansiedge-micro-mark-e-transparent.png at height 16px (right), mzansiedge.co.za · Bet responsibly · 18+ on the left.
  • Banned: emoji prefix on the count, orange-gradient tick (only the brand bar uses gradient), wordmark text fallback when the logo image is available.
