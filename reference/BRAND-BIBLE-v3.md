## 01 · Brand Essence

**As passionate as fire. As fast as lightning.**

MzansiEdge is South Africa's AI-powered sports betting intelligence platform. We cross-reference odds from all major SA bookmakers, live player form and injury data, tipster consensus from multiple prediction sources, and real-time match conditions — all to find the moments where the bookmakers got it wrong.

Our brand is the visual embodiment of that edge: razor-sharp accuracy wrapped in the energy and warmth of South African sporting culture. We are bold without being reckless. Confident without being arrogant. Data-driven but never cold.

## 05 · Logo Clear Space

Clear space is measured using the height of the `E` lightning bolt in the wordmark as the base unit (`1×`). Minimum exclusion: **1× E-height on all sides.**

### Minimum Size Requirements

| Variant | Digital Min | Print Min | Context |
|---------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Primary Logo | 120px wide | 30mm | Hero sections, formal presentations |
| Wordmark | 80px wide | 20mm | Headers, navigation, compact spaces |
| Micro Mark E | 16px wide | 4mm | Favicons, app icons only |
| B.R.U. Avatar | 40px wide | 10mm | Social profile images |

### Responsive Logo Behaviour

| Breakpoint | Logo |
|------------|------|
| Desktop (1200px+) | Primary logo with slogan |
| Tablet (768–1199px) | Wordmark only (no slogan) |
| Mobile (320–767px) | Wordmark or Micro Mark E |
| Favicon / Tab (<32px) | Micro Mark E only |

## 06 · Logo Misuse — DON'Ts

- **Stretched / distorted** — never alter the logo's proportions.
- **Rotated / tilted** — the logo must always sit level.
- **Flat orange fill** — never replace the gradient with solid orange.
- **Recoloured** — never change the logo to non-brand colours.
- **On busy background** — avoid clashing or noisy backgrounds.
- **Drop shadow added** — never add effects not in the original.
- **Logo recreated in type** — never try to rebuild with any font.
- **B.R.U. replacing logo** — B.R.U. is secondary, never primary.
- **Orange background fill** — orange is accent only, never bg fill.
- **Insufficient clear space** — maintain 1× E-height clearance.
- **Outline / stroke version** — never reduce to outline only.
- **Paired with micro mark** — never use full logo + micro mark together.

**Correct usage:** Always use the supplied logo files exactly as provided. Match the logo variant to the background context. Use transparent variants on existing backgrounds. Use monochrome where gradients aren't practical.

| Family | Weight | Role |
|--------|--------|------|
| **Outfit Bold** | Bold | Display / headers, hero text, section titles. Bold, geometric, modern. |
| **Work Sans** | Regular / SemiBold | Body copy, descriptions, UI text. Clean, readable, professional. |

### Type Scale

| Level | Font | Size | Weight | Line-height | Tracking | Usage |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|----------|-------|
| H1 | Outfit | 32pt | Bold | 1.2× | -0.02em | Hero headlines |
| H2 | Outfit | 24pt | Bold | 1.3× | 0 | Section titles |
| H3 | Outfit | 18pt | Bold | 1.35× | 0.01em | Subsection headers |
| Body | Work Sans | 14pt | Regular | 1.6× | 0 | Paragraphs, descriptions |
| Small | Work Sans | 12pt | Regular | 1.5× | 0.01em | Captions, meta text |
| Mono | Geist Mono | 13pt | Regular | 1.4× | 0 | Data, odds, statistics |
| Label | Work Sans | 11pt | SemiBold | 1.3× | 0.05em | Buttons, tags, badges |

### Typography Rules

- Never use more than two typefaces in a single layout (Outfit + Work Sans).
- Geist Mono is reserved for data, stats, and technical content only.
- Minimum body text size: 14pt (web), 10pt (print).
- Line height: 1.5× for body, 1.2× for headers.
- **SA English spelling: realise, favour, analyse, colour. Always.**
- The logo typeface is custom — never recreate it in Outfit or any other font.
- All-caps usage: limited to labels, badges, and tier names. Never for body text.

## 08 · Voice & Tone

**We sound like a sharp mate who knows the numbers.**

MzansiEdge speaks with quiet confidence backed by data. We never shout, never oversell, and never promise what we can't deliver. But when we do speak, people listen — because we've done the homework and the numbers are on our side.

### Voice Spectrum (we sit in the middle-to-right)

Authoritative ↔ Friendly · Formal ↔ Conversational · Serious ↔ Fun · Verbose ↔ Concise · Technical ↔ Accessible

### Brand Voice Qualities

| Is | Is Not | Example |
|----|--------|---------|
| Confident | not arrogant | "We earned this. The data is clear." |
| Passionate | not reckless | "We love the game — and we respect the risk." |
| Sharp | not cold | "Precision with personality. Data with soul." |
| Fun | not frivolous | "We can crack a joke. The maths still checks out." |
| Honest | not blunt | "We show the losses alongside the wins." |

## 13 · Responsible Gambling

MzansiEdge has a legal and ethical obligation to promote responsible gambling. These standards are non-negotiable.

### Core Principles

- **Honesty over hype** — we never exaggerate returns or downplay risk. Every edge comes with uncertainty.
- **Transparency in losses** — we report misses with the same prominence as wins. Season accuracy is always shown.
- **No pressure selling** — after 3+ consecutive losses, upgrade CTAs are suppressed for 48 hours automatically.
- **Data speaks first** — let numbers and track records build credibility. Never use emotional pressure.
- **Audience protection** — no content targeting minors. No content encouraging betting beyond one's means.

### Mandatory Footer

Required on: monthly reports, trial messages, re-engagement nudges, any content showing betting results.

> Betting involves risk. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
> MzansiEdge provides data-driven intelligence, not financial advice.
> Please gamble responsibly. National Responsible Gambling Programme: 0800 006 008.

**Channel exception (per SO #34, 13 Apr 2026):** Do NOT include this footer on IG/TikTok/WA Channel/FB/LinkedIn social captions — they hurt algorithmic reach and are NOT legally required there. Telegram and WhatsApp group use `18+ · Play Responsibly` only. Full rule in `ops/CONTENT-LAWS.md`.

### Language Rules for Betting Content

| Context | ✓ Approved | ✗ Banned |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Describing an edge | `Value opportunity`, `positive EV` | `Sure bet`, `guaranteed profit` |
| Win notification | `Our analysis was correct` | `We called it!`, `Easy money` |
| Loss notification | `Market was right on this one` | `Bad luck`, `Unlucky break` |
| Accuracy claims | `58% season accuracy (verified)` | `We rarely miss`, `Almost always right` |
| Upgrade pitch | `See more edges with Diamond` | `Win more with Diamond` |
| General CTA | `Find your edge`, `Bet. Better.` | `Start winning today` |

### Notification Display Rules

- Show losses with same visual prominence as wins. Never hide or minimise.
- Include season accuracy percentage in all results communications.
- 3+ consecutive misses → suppress upgrade CTAs for 48 hours.
- Quiet confidence tone. Let numbers speak. One emoji per section maximum.
- Monthly reports must include responsible gambling footer.
